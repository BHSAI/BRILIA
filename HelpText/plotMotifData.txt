plotMotifData will plot the AID and ADAR motifs and the resulting X->Y
nucleotide subsitution frequencies. 

  INPUT
    MotifData: Structure of Structure of hostpost motifs, where the values
      within a struture containins a 1x4 matrix of mutation frequency from
      X to A, C, G, or T in that order.
    Type ['syn', 'nonsyn', 'all']: to plot synonymous, nonsynonymous, or
      both types of mutations

  OUTPUT
    Gx: figure handle
    Axs: figures handles of the subplots
